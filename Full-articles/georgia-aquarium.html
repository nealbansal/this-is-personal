Georgia Aquarium

Atlanta, GA

You will never see fish the same again after you visit the Georgia Aquarium. Home to the most aquatic life than any other aquarium, it has fun stuff for all ages. The aquarium houses several exhibits throughout the aquarium, looking at marine life from all different backgrounds. You can see the alligators in the swamps or the different jellyfish across the ocean. You can also be entertained by a dolphin show or get splashed at the sea lion show. But if you are tired of looking at the varieties of fish, you can have a whole new experience and jump right in with them.
